Village style baked potatoes

Contrary to what you might expect, tasty potatoes in the oven is one of the most delicious dishes in the world. So check out this country-style recipe we`ve prepared for you

Ingridients

  • potatoes - 4.5 lb (2 kg) fresh, small
  • oil - 1/2 cup
  • salt - 1 tsp
  • savory - 2 tsp
  • paprika - 1 tsp
  • black pepper - 1 tsp
  • dill - 1 tsp, dry
  • parsley - to taste, dry
  • garlic - 1 tsp, powdered
  • beer - 1/2 cup, light

Steps

  1. Clean the potatoes and halve them without peeling them. Put them to boil in 1/2 cup beer until semi-cooked (they must remain hard).
  2. Drain the potatoes of water and allow them to cool slightly. In a large bowl, mix all the spices and pour the potatoes in. Shake the bowl so that the spices get evenly distributed over the potatoes.
  3. In a tray, heat the oil (but not too much) and place the potatoes, then gently mix. Bake the potatoes in a moderately preheated oven to an appetizing light tan.
